About the Family room

The Family Room is our largest single-room layout — one queen bed plus three single beds, all in the same open-plan space, with a private ensuite, air conditioning, flat-screen TV, fridge, kettle and free Wi-Fi. Sleeps up to five. It's the room a family of four or five books when they want to be together, near the beach, without paying $400+ a night for a serviced apartment.

It's an upstairs room — stairs to your door, no lift. Honest about that upfront because it matters when you're carrying a tired toddler and three suitcases. If stairs would make day one a problem, our Triple Ground Floor sleeps three on the ground level. Otherwise, this is the family room you want.

Who it's for

Two parents and two or three children aged 6 and over. School holiday trips. Visiting family on the Gold Coast for a long weekend. The kind of trip where the kids spend the day at the beach or the pool and crash early enough that the adults get a quiet hour with the door closed. Multi-adult groups happy to share an open-plan room — three friends plus two extras, two couples on a budget weekend, in-laws who don't mind close quarters.

What it's not for: families with babies or toddlers who need a cot. We don't supply cots, rollaways or extra bedding for any room — bedding is fixed at one queen plus three singles. If you need a cot, you'd need to bring one (folding travel cots fit) or look elsewhere. Children 6 and over are charged as adults at our motel.

The honest version of "family room"

"Family Room" gets used for everything from a 12-square-metre studio to a three-bedroom suite. Here's what ours is: a single open-plan room large enough for four beds and a family of up to five, with one bathroom, in a clean and well-maintained motel five minutes from the beach. There's no kitchenette beyond a fridge and kettle. There's no separate bedroom or play area. There's one TV. If your family wants a self-contained apartment with a kitchen and a separate kids' bedroom, look at holiday apartments in Burleigh Heads — they're great, just three to four times the price. If your family wants to be on the coast, near the beach, sharing a clean room and spending the saved money on the trip itself, the Family Room is built for that.

Frequently Asked

FAMILY ROOM — Q&A

How many people fit in the Family Room?

Up to five — one queen bed and three single beds. Two parents plus three kids works, two parents plus two kids works comfortably, and so does a couple plus three older children. Children 6 and over are charged as adults.

Are there cots, rollaways or extra beds?

No — bedding is fixed at one queen plus three singles. We don't supply cots, rollaways or extra beds for any room.

If you need a cot for a baby, please check with reception before booking — folding travel cots fit in the room, but you'd need to bring your own. We can't take infants if there's no safe sleeping arrangement.

Is there a separate kids' room?

No — the Family Room is one open-plan space with all four beds in it. If you need a separate kids' bedroom, our Interconnecting Rooms are two adjoining rooms with two ensuites — typically the better option for families who need separated sleeping space.

Is the pool fenced and safe for kids?

Yes — the outdoor pool is fenced and gated to Queensland safety standards. It's open all year and free for guests. Children must be supervised by an adult at all times — there's no lifeguard.

How far to the beach with kids?

Miami Beach is 400 metres, around 5 minutes' walk on flat footpath. Genuinely walkable with kids and beach gear — no need to drive. The motel is 200m from Burleigh Beach (a flat 6-minute walk). Both are patrolled in season.

Can we self-cater for the family?

Partially. The room has a bar fridge, kettle and microwave but no cooktop. Aldi is directly across the road, so groceries are easy. The microwave handles reheating leftovers, warming kids' meals or heating up takeaway from the bakery or local cafés to keep food costs down.

For full self-catering you'd want a holiday apartment with a kitchen, but for breakfast supplies, snacks and reheating leftovers in the microwave, our setup works fine.

Is parking available for the family car?

Yes — free on-site parking, one permit per room, subject to availability. Earlier arrivals get the better odds. Free street parking is also available outside the motel.

Can I book the Family Room directly?

Yes — book through our website or call (07) 5576 3211. No platform fees, no resort fees. Direct booking with guaranteed courtyard parking and instant confirmation.
