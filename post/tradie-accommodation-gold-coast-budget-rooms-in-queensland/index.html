What Tradies Actually Need

Most accommodation categories are built around leisure travellers, and the infrastructure reflects that. A tradie working on the Gold Coast — whether for a few nights or a few weeks — has a different set of priorities. The list is shorter, and the gaps between adequate and inadequate accommodation are clearer when you're arriving at 5:30pm, dirty and tired, with an early start the next day.

The Ground Floor Room — No Stairs

The Triple Ground Floor room is the standout option for tradies who have been on their feet all day and do not want to carry gear up stairs. It sits on the ground level with direct access from the car park. The room has a queen bed plus a single bed (sleeps 2–3) and the same full inclusions as every other room: private ensuite, reverse-cycle AC, ceiling fan, flat-screen TV, microwave, bar fridge, kettle and free Wi-Fi.

This is also the preferred room for guests with limited mobility, older travellers, and anyone arriving with heavy luggage or equipment. The car can park directly in front of the room in most cases, which makes loading and unloading straightforward.

Free Parking — Including Utes and Vans

On-site parking is free and included with every room. Standard passenger vehicles and most standard-cab utes fit the parking bays without issue. The motel is on the Gold Coast Highway in Miami, which has direct access north and south without the narrow side-street issues common to accommodation closer to the beach.

For larger vehicles — long-wheelbase utes, dual-cab utes, vans or trades vehicles with trailers — call ahead on (07) 5576 3211 to confirm availability and discuss the best parking arrangement. The team can advise on suitable spaces before you arrive.

Room Options — For Solo Tradies and Crews

Whether you're a solo tradie or a crew of four or five on a bigger job, there's a room that fits without forcing you to book two separate rooms and pay for two. The double room covers solo and pairs; the triples and quad cover small crews. For larger crews, the interconnecting rooms give two connected ensuited rooms under one booking.

Room Inclusions — All Standard, No Extras

Every room at Burleigh Gold Coast Motel has a private ensuite with shower, reverse-cycle air conditioning, ceiling fan, flat-screen TV, microwave, bar fridge, kettle, free Wi-Fi and pool access. There are no "premium" room tiers that gate the basics behind a higher rate — these inclusions are standard across all six room types. The pool is available to use whenever you want, and towels are provided.

Location — Gold Coast Highway, Miami QLD

The motel is at 1908–1910 Gold Coast Highway, Corner Christine Avenue, Miami QLD 4220. Miami is one suburb north of Burleigh Heads on the main coast highway. The Gold Coast highway connects directly to the M1 motorway north to Brisbane (about 80km, 55–65 minutes) and south to Coolangatta (22km, 20 minutes). The location puts you central to the Gold Coast's southern half — well positioned for work sites between Robina, Southport, Broadbeach, Mermaid Beach, Burleigh, Tallebudgera and Currumbin.

Long Stays and Multi-Week Bookings

For workers needing accommodation for a week or more, the best approach is to call the motel directly on (07) 5576 3211 and discuss availability and multi-night rates. Extended stays are accommodated where rooms are available, and the team can work with flexible check-out arrangements for workers who don't have a fixed end date on a contract.

The motel's self-contained rooms — with microwave, bar fridge, kettle and private ensuite — make extended stays considerably more comfortable than a hotel room without cooking facilities. A room with a bar fridge and microwave is the difference between spending $15 on breakfast from the room and $28 at a café every single morning over a two-week job.
