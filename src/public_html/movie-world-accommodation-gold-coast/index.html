Why stay at Burleigh for a Movie World trip

Movie World sits at Oxenford — 30 minutes north of the motel up the M1. Park-side resorts and the big hotel names near Oxenford charge $300–$450 per night in peak season. We start at $125 a night, with free on-site parking, no resort fees, and no booking platform surcharges. On a three-night family trip the difference is typically $500–$900, which covers a lot of park tickets.

Burleigh is also a better base for the rest of the trip. You're 400 metres from Miami Beach and 200m from Burleigh Beach (a flat 6-minute walk), 200 metres from buses and the Gold Coast tram, and surrounded by the kind of restaurants, cafés and beach paths that make the non-park days just as good as the park days.

Getting to Movie World from the motel

Head north on the Gold Coast Highway from the motel, join the M1 at Burleigh, and exit at Exit 60 (Oxenford / Helensvale). Movie World is signposted from the exit. From motel forecourt to the park gates is around 30 minutes off-peak.

On school holidays and Saturday mornings, add 10–20 minutes. If you want to be at the gates near the 9:30am opening time, leave the motel by 8:45am on a normal day, 8:30am on a holiday.

Parking at Movie World is free in the general car park — a short walk from the entrance gates. There's a paid premium option closer to the gate, but most people don't bother.

Planning a full day at Movie World

Movie World is a full-day park. Most families arrive at opening and stay until the 5:00pm close — there's genuinely that much to do. The headline coasters are DC Rivals HyperCoaster, Superman Escape, Green Lantern and Doomsday Destroyer. Younger kids are well served by Looney Tunes Village and the Justice League dark ride. There's a Hollywood stunt-driver show daily and rolling character meets throughout the day.

The honest bit: The 30-minute drive each way is the trade-off vs staying park-side. For most families, that 60 minutes per day is absolutely worth what they save on accommodation — particularly for a 3- or 4-night stay. If your group needs to be at the park gates the moment they open every single day, park-side makes more sense.

Tickets and multi-park passes

Buy tickets from the Village Roadshow Theme Parks website before you arrive. Gate prices are noticeably higher than online prices. The Mega Pass covers Movie World, Sea World and Wet'n'Wild on unlimited entry and pays for itself on a stay of more than two park days.

We don't sell tickets at reception and don't take commission on park tickets. This is genuinely the best advice.

MOVIE WORLD ACCOMMODATION — Q&A

How far is the motel from Movie World?

Around 30 minutes by car off-peak — north on the Gold Coast Highway from the motel, then the M1 to Exit 60 (Oxenford / Helensvale). Movie World is signposted from the exit. Allow an extra 10–20 minutes on school holidays and Saturday mornings.

How much do we save compared to staying near Movie World?

Park-side resorts run $300–$450 per night in peak season. Our rooms start from $125/night. On a three-night family stay, that's typically $500–$900 back — usually enough to cover extra park tickets, meals out, and spending money for the kids.

There are no resort fees, no booking platform fees and no parking charges at the motel.

Is parking free at the motel and at Movie World?

Yes to both. Motel parking is free for guests (one permit per room, subject to availability — earlier arrivals get the better spots). Movie World has a free general car park a short walk from the entrance gates.

Which room is best for a family of four doing Movie World?

The Quad Room — queen bed plus two singles — sleeps four comfortably from $185/night. If you need more space or have five or six people, the Interconnecting Rooms give you two full rooms and two bathrooms from $295/night.

Where should we buy Movie World tickets?

Directly from the Village Roadshow Theme Parks website. Online prices are noticeably cheaper than gate prices. If you're doing Movie World plus Sea World or Wet'n'Wild, the multi-park Mega Pass is almost always better value than individual day tickets.
