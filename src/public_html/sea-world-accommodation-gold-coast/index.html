Why stay at Burleigh for a Sea World trip

Sea World is on Sea World Drive at Main Beach — 25 minutes north of the motel via the Gold Coast Highway. Sea World Resort, which sits literally next door to the park, runs $300+ per night in peak season. Our rooms start at $125/night with free parking, no resort fees and no booking surcharges. The 25-minute drive each way is a reasonable trade for that saving.

Burleigh also gives you a better base for the whole Gold Coast trip. You're 400 metres from Miami Beach, surrounded by beaches, restaurants and the coastal walking paths that make Burleigh and Miami worth visiting in their own right. The park-side hotels put you in a tourist precinct — fine for the park day, but thin on everything else.

Getting to Sea World from the motel

Head north on the Gold Coast Highway through Mermaid Beach, Broadbeach, and Surfers Paradise. Continue north to Sea World Drive at Main Beach and follow the signs. The drive is straightforward — mostly highway the whole way. Expect 25 minutes off-peak, and allow 35–40 minutes on school holidays or weekend mornings.

Parking at Sea World is free and on-site, a short walk to the entrance. The motel parking is also free for guests.

What Sea World offers

Sea World is the most marine-focused of the Gold Coast parks — dolphin encounters, shark experiences, ray feeds, the Shark Bay habitat walk, and a solid collection of rides including Jet Rescue and the Storm coaster. For younger kids, Nickelodeon Land with SpongeBob and friends is very well done.

The dolphin interactions are the standout for most families — you can watch from the grandstand at the lagoon shows or book into a closer experience where guests get into the water with the animals. These sell out, so book online before you arrive.

Sea World Cruises runs separately — catamaran whale-watching (seasonal) and island day trips departing from the Sea World wharf. These are bookable through the Sea World website and worth considering if you have a spare half-day.

Worth knowing: Sea World is typically a 6–8 hour park day — substantial but slightly shorter than a Movie World day for most families. If you're combining parks, Sea World pairs naturally with a morning or afternoon at nearby Broadbeach or Surfers Paradise, both a short drive south on the way back to the motel.

SEA WORLD ACCOMMODATION — Q&A

How far is the motel from Sea World?

Around 25 minutes off-peak — north on the Gold Coast Highway through Broadbeach and Surfers Paradise to Sea World Drive at Main Beach. Allow 35–40 minutes on school holidays and weekend mornings.

How much do we save vs Sea World Resort?

Sea World Resort runs $300+ per night in peak season. Our rooms start from $125/night. On a three-night family stay, that's $500+ back — plus there are no resort fees, no parking charges, and no booking platform fees.

Do we need to book the dolphin experiences in advance?

Yes. The in-water dolphin interaction experiences sell out well in advance during school holidays. Book through the Sea World website before you travel, not when you arrive at the park.

Which room suits a family visiting Sea World?

The Family Room (queen + 3 singles, sleeps 5) from $195/night is the most-booked for families. For four guests, the Quad Room from $185/night is a better fit. For groups of 5–6, the Interconnecting Rooms give two full rooms and two bathrooms from $295/night.

Can we combine Sea World with Movie World on the same trip?

Yes — and the Village Roadshow Mega Pass covers Sea World, Movie World and Wet'n'Wild on unlimited entry. If you're doing more than one park it almost always works out cheaper than separate tickets. Buy online before you arrive.
