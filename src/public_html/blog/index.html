QUICK ANSWERS

Things readers ask most when planning a Gold Coast trip.

How far is Burleigh from the theme parks?
Movie World & Wet 'n' Wild are 26 minutes north on the M1. Sea World is 29 minutes. Dreamworld is 32 minutes. Currumbin Wildlife Sanctuary is just 10 minutes south. Same highway, easy drive — see our theme park guide.
Is Burleigh better than Surfers Paradise for families?
For most families — yes. Burleigh is quieter, has a national park headland, calmer beaches, and direct beach access without the high-rise crowds. We're 10 minutes drive to Surfers if you want a night out, and 10 minutes south to Coolangatta. Best of both ends.
What's the cheapest way to stay on the Gold Coast?
Book direct, avoid park-front resorts, and stay slightly off the strip. Our rooms start at $125/night with no booking fees, no resort fees, and free parking. Check live availability.
When are the busiest periods?
QLD school holidays (Easter, June/July, September, December), Sea World Carnivale (Feb–March), Gold Coast Film Festival (April–May), Cooly Rocks On (June), and Supercars weekend (October). Peak rooms fill 4–8 weeks ahead.
Can you walk to the beach?
Miami Beach is a 5-minute walk (400m). Burleigh Beach with its iconic headland is 200m down the highway. James Street cafes and dining strip are 10–12 minutes walk.
Do you have parking? How much is it?
Free on-site parking, one permit per room, subject to availability. Big enough for utes, vans and trailers. Compare that to $30/night at park-front hotels.
