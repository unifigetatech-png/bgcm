QUICK ANSWERS

Most-asked questions before guests arrive.

What time is check-in and check-out?
Check-in is from 2:00pm. Check-out is by 10:00am. Reception is open 9am–7:30pm daily — if you're arriving outside reception hours, please call ahead so we can arrange access.
Is parking free?
Yes — free on-site parking, one vehicle per room, subject to availability. Big enough for utes, vans and trailers. Let us know at booking if you have a larger vehicle.
Do all rooms have a private bathroom?
Yes. Every room has its own private ensuite — no shared facilities. See our six room types.
How far is it to the beach?
Miami Beach is approximately a 5-minute walk (400m). Burleigh Beach is 200m from the motel (a flat 6-minute walk).
What's the best way to book?
Book direct through our website for instant confirmation, guaranteed courtyard parking, and no third-party markup. You can also call reception on (07) 5576 3211 between 9am and 7:30pm daily.
Are pets allowed?
We don't currently accommodate pets. Contact reception if you have specific needs and we'll do our best to advise on the right room or alternative.
Can I bring a larger group or interconnecting rooms?
Yes — we have an interconnecting room that sleeps 6+ across two rooms with two bathrooms. For larger groups, send a quick message via the form above and we'll work out the right combination.
