About the Quad room

The Quad is the practical option for four people who don't mind sharing a single open-plan room. One queen bed and two single beds, all in the same space, with a private ensuite, air conditioning, flat-screen TV, fridge, kettle and free Wi-Fi. It's an upstairs room — stairs only, no lift — and it sleeps four comfortably without the cost of two separate rooms.

If you need separated sleeping space — say a couple plus older kids who'd rather not share a wall — our Interconnecting Rooms are the better fit. The Quad is for groups happy to be in the same room, and it prices accordingly at $185 a night.

Who it's for

Three or four mates on a Gold Coast trip — surfers, hens or bucks weekends without the chaos, mates flying in for a music festival, golf weekends. Two adults plus one or two older children — kids 6 and over are charged as adults at our motel, and the Quad gives them their own beds rather than sharing the queen. Three or four work colleagues sharing accommodation on a budget. Couples travelling with adult parents or in-laws who don't mind the proximity.

What it's not for: families with young children who need a quiet space to put kids to bed early. The open-plan layout means everyone shares the same lights, the same TV time, and the same bedtime. If that's a problem, the Family Room is the same broad layout but designed around that being the room's whole job.

The honest version of "sleeps four"

"Sleeps four" is one of those phrases hotels and motels use loosely. Here's what it actually means at our place: four adults can absolutely sleep in this room, and people do, regularly. But four full-sized adults in one open-plan motel room with one bathroom is intimate. Bags get parked. Someone showers while someone else is making coffee. The TV is shared. If your group is comfortable with that, the Quad is exactly what you booked. If your group needs personal space, look at the Interconnecting Rooms instead — two adjoining rooms with two ensuites for $295/night total.

Frequently Asked

QUAD ROOM — Q&A

Are the four beds in the same room?

Yes. The Quad is one open-plan room with a queen bed and two single beds, plus the ensuite. There's no separate bedroom or partition. If you need separated sleeping space, our Interconnecting Rooms are two adjoining rooms with two ensuites — better suited to groups who need their own space.

Can four adults realistically share this room?

Yes — and people do, every week. It's a real motel room layout designed for four sleepers, not a tight squeeze. That said, four adults sharing one open-plan room with one bathroom is intimate. If your group is comfortable with that — mates on a trip, family travelling close — the Quad is great value at $185. If your group needs more space, the Interconnecting Rooms cost more but solve that.

Is there only one bathroom for four people?

Yes — one private ensuite. The room is set up so four people sleep in it; the bathroom is single-use, shared between you. If a second bathroom matters for your group, the Interconnecting Rooms have two ensuites.

Are kids welcome in the Quad?

Yes — children 6 and over are charged as adults at our motel and count toward the room's four-person capacity. We don't have cots or rollaways for any room, and we don't take infants in cots. If you're travelling with babies, please call reception before booking to talk through what we can and can't do.

Can I park multiple cars?

One free parking permit per room is included, subject to availability. We can't promise space for two or three vehicles in the on-site lot. Free street parking is available outside the motel on Christine Avenue and the Gold Coast Highway — most groups split between on-site and street with no issues.

How far to James Street and Burleigh nightlife?

James Street is a 5-minute drive — that's the bars, the cafés, the night-out strip. We're far enough from it for quiet sleep, close enough for a quick Uber back. Walking it from Miami is doable but takes around 25 minutes.

Can I book the Quad directly?

Yes. Book through our website or call (07) 5576 3211. No platform fees, no resort fees. Direct booking with guaranteed courtyard parking and instant confirmation.
