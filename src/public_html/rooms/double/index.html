About the double room

The Double Room is the most-booked layout at the Burleigh Gold Coast Motel, and there's a simple reason — it does what a good motel room should do. One queen bed with fresh linen, a private ensuite with a strong shower, air conditioning that actually works, a flat-screen TV, a bar fridge, a kettle with tea and coffee, and free Wi-Fi. Nothing flashy. Just clean, honest motel comfort, five minutes' walk from Miami Beach and ten from Burleigh Heads.

This is an upstairs room. We don't have a lift — there are stairs to your door, and we want to be upfront about that before you book. Our upper-floor rooms are also typically the quietest in the property, sitting further from check-in arrivals and the road below. Road-facing rooms are fitted with double-glazed windows for noise and heat insulation. If stairs are an issue, our Triple Ground Floor room is the only no-stairs option at the motel — same comforts, ground level.

Who it's for

This room sells out first because it covers the broadest set of needs. Couples on a coast getaway who want to be near the beach without paying Burleigh Heads pricing. Solo travellers driving up the coast who need a clean bed and a parking spot. Business travellers passing through who'd rather pay $125 than $295 for somewhere to sleep before a meeting on the M1. People visiting family in Miami, Mermaid or Burleigh who want their own space.

What it's not built for: families. A queen bed is a queen bed — it's two adults, not two adults and a child. Children 6 and over are charged as adults at our motel, and we don't supply cots or rollaways. If you're travelling with kids, look at our Triple Upstairs, Quad or Family rooms instead.

The location, in honest distances

The motel sits at 1908–1910 Gold Coast Highway in Miami, on the corner of Christine Avenue. From your door:

  • Miami Beach — 400 metres, around 5 minutes' walk. Your nearest swim. Quieter than Burleigh, great for sunsets.
  • Burleigh Beach — 200m from the motel (a flat 6-minute walk). Patrolled, family-friendly, the iconic one with the headland behind it.
  • Paddock Bakery — 150 metres. Walk out the door and you're there.
  • Aldi Miami — directly across the road. Useful if you're self-catering.
  • Burleigh Head National Park — 2.9 km. Headland walk with wildlife, easy half-day trip.
  • Gold Coast Airport — around 15–25 minutes by car depending on traffic. Route 777 bus runs from a stop nearby (a short walk on the Gold Coast Highway), about 25 minutes — fares from 50c with a Go Card.
  • Surfers Paradise — 12 km north, around 20 minutes' drive.

What's included in your booking

Every Double Room booking includes free use of the outdoor pool (open all year, fenced, gated), coin-operated laundry, luggage storage at reception, and free on-site parking subject to availability — one permit per room. Daily housekeeping is included for stays of three nights or more. For shorter stays, we leave you to it; daily service is available on request at a small extra charge.

Honest heads up — before you book this room:
  • Upstairs only — stairs to your room, no lift.
  • Maximum two guests. No cots, no rollaways, no extra beds.
  • $200 AUD damage deposit on arrival by credit card. Refunded within 7 days subject to inspection.
  • Card surcharge 1.65% + 30¢. Visa and Mastercard only — no Amex.
  • Check-in 2:00pm–7:00pm. If arriving after 5pm, please let reception know in advance.

Frequently Asked

DOUBLE ROOM — Q&A

Is the double room on the ground floor?

No. Our Double Rooms are on the upper floor and reached by stairs. There is no lift in the building.

If you need ground-floor access, our Triple Ground Floor is the only no-stairs room at the motel. Same private ensuite, same comforts — just on the ground level.

How many people can sleep in the Double Room?

Two adults. The Double Room has one queen bed and is designed for couples or solo travellers. We don't provide cots, rollaways or extra bedding for any of our rooms.

If you need to sleep three or more, look at our Triple Upstairs, Quad or Family rooms.

Does the Double Room have a bath?

No — the private ensuite is a shower, toilet and basin. There are no baths in any of our rooms.

Is parking included with the Double Room?

Yes — free on-site parking, one permit per room, subject to availability. Earlier arrivals get the better odds. Street parking is also available outside the motel on Christine Avenue and the Gold Coast Highway, both untimed in this section.

How far is the beach from the Double Room?

Miami Beach is a 5-minute walk (around 400 metres) from the motel — the nearest beach. 200m from Burleigh Beach (a flat 6-minute walk). Both are patrolled in season. Miami is quieter; Burleigh has the headland and the cafés.

Is the Wi-Fi free, and is it fast enough for streaming?

Wi-Fi is free in every room. It works well for general use — emails, browsing, maps, social media. It can be slow for streaming, especially in the evening when the motel is full.

The TV is local free-to-air channels only — no streaming apps like Netflix, Stan or Foxtel. If you need to stream, your phone or laptop on the Wi-Fi will do the job most of the time, but we'd be lying if we said it was always fast.

What time can I check in and check out?

Check-in is from 2:00pm to 7:00pm. If you'll arrive after 5pm, please let reception know in advance so we can have your key ready. After-hours arrivals can be arranged with notice.

Check-out is by 10:00am. We can store luggage at reception if your flight or plans run later in the day.

Can I book the Double Room directly without a booking platform?

Yes — and we'd recommend it. Booking direct through our website or by calling reception on (07) 5576 3211 is the cheapest way to stay. There are no booking platform fees and no resort fees added to the rate. If you find a lower public rate elsewhere, call us and we'll match it.
