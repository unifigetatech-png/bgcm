Before You Book

A FEW HONEST THINGS
WORTH KNOWING

We'd rather tell you upfront than have you find out at check-in. None of these are deal-breakers — they're just how this motel works.

🪜 No lift

All rooms except the Triple Ground Floor are upstairs and reached by stairs. If stairs are an issue, choose the Triple Ground Floor or call us before booking.

🛏️ No cots, no extra beds

Bedding is fixed per room type. We don't add rollaways or cots. Children 6 and over are charged as adults.

🚗 Parking — subject to availability

Free on-site parking, one permit per room — but space is not guaranteed. Earlier arrivals get the better odds. Street parking is also available outside the motel.

📺 TV — local channels only

Flat-screen TV in every room with local free-to-air channels. No streaming services or smart-TV apps. Wi-Fi is free for general browsing — works fine for emails and maps, can be slow for streaming.

💳 Damage deposit & surcharge

$200 AUD damage deposit on arrival by credit card, refunded within 7 days subject to inspection. Card surcharge 1.65% + 30¢. Visa and Mastercard only — no Amex.

🕒 Reception hours & check-in

Reception 9am–7:30pm daily. Check-in 2:00pm–7:00pm — please let us know in advance if you'll arrive after 5pm. Check-out by 10:00am. Minimum check-in age: 18.

🧹 Housekeeping

Daily service is included for stays of 3 nights or more. For shorter stays, we leave you to it — daily service is available on request at a small extra charge.

🐶 No pets · No smoking · No parties

We're not pet-friendly, the property is non-smoking throughout, and we don't host parties or events. Quiet enjoyment for all guests is the rule.
