Why stay at Burleigh for a Wet'n'Wild trip

Wet'n'Wild Gold Coast sits at Oxenford, 30 minutes north of the motel on the M1 — the same exit as Movie World. There are no park-side hotels immediately next to Wet'n'Wild, so most visitors staying near the park end up at Oxenford hotels or the big resort strip at Surfers Paradise. Both options cost more than us and neither gets you closer to the beach.

Our rooms start at $125/night with free on-site parking and no fees of any kind. You're also 400 metres from Miami Beach and 200m from Burleigh Beach (a flat 6-minute walk) — which makes the morning after a Wet'n'Wild day a lot better than the equivalent from an Oxenford hotel.

The motel also has its own outdoor pool. After a day on water slides, coming back to a quiet pool in the courtyard is a pattern a lot of families repeat on multi-night stays.

Getting to Wet'n'Wild from the motel

Head north on the Gold Coast Highway, join the M1 at Burleigh, and exit at Exit 60 (Oxenford / Helensvale). Wet'n'Wild and Movie World share the same exit and the same general car park area — you can see both parks from the car park. From the motel to the park gates is around 30 minutes off-peak. Allow 40 minutes on school holidays and weekend mornings.

Parking at Wet'n'Wild is free.

What a Wet'n'Wild day looks like

Wet'n'Wild is a half to full-day park depending on your group. The wave pool and lazy river are the easy defaults — low queue, very relaxed, good for all ages. The speed slides and tube rides have longer waits during peak but move fast once you're queued. The main family ride is the multi-person raft attraction, which most kids will ride five or six times.

For teenagers and adults, the Black Hole and Tornado are the headline thrill rides. For young kids, the shallow pool areas and smaller slides near the wave pool are the focus.

Most groups are done by 3–4pm. The park is at its busiest between 11am and 2pm — arrive at opening (10am) and you'll do your best rides in the first two hours before the queues build.

Sunscreen note: Wet'n'Wild is almost entirely outdoors with very little shade. The sun hits hard between 10am and 3pm, even in autumn. Pack reef-safe sunscreen and reapply every hour — the water washes it off faster than you think. Kids in rashies is the smart move.

Combining with Movie World

Since Movie World and Wet'n'Wild share the same M1 exit, many visitors do both parks on a multi-night stay. The Village Roadshow Mega Pass covers both parks (plus Sea World) on unlimited entry — almost always better value than buying separate tickets for each day.

Frequently Asked

WET'N'WILD ACCOMMODATION — Q&A

How far is the motel from Wet'n'Wild?

Around 30 minutes off-peak — north on the Gold Coast Highway, then the M1 to Exit 60 (Oxenford). Wet'n'Wild is at the same exit as Movie World. Allow 40 minutes on school holidays and weekend mornings.

Does the motel have a pool?

Yes — an outdoor pool with sun lounges in the courtyard. It's a good size for a motel pool. After a day at Wet'n'Wild, the motel pool in the evening is a popular routine for families staying multiple nights.

Can we do Wet'n'Wild and Movie World on the same trip?

Yes — they share Exit 60 off the M1 and effectively the same car park area at Oxenford. Most families do one park per day, which is the right call. The Village Roadshow Mega Pass covers both parks plus Sea World on unlimited entry — almost always better value than individual tickets.

What should we bring to Wet'n'Wild?

Reef-safe sunscreen (reapply every hour — the water strips it). Rashies for the kids. Thongs for the hot paths between attractions. A waterproof phone pouch. Lockers are available on-site to hire — worth it for phones and valuables on the big slides.

You can leave the park and re-enter same day if you want to grab food outside and avoid the in-park food prices.

Which room is best for a family of four or five?

For four, the Quad Room (queen + 2 singles) from $185/night is the right fit. For five, the Family Room (queen + 3 singles) from $195/night. For groups of 5–6, the Interconnecting Rooms give two full rooms and two bathrooms from $295/night.
