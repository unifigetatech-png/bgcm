About the Motel

Burleigh Gold Coast Motel sits on the Gold Coast Highway at the corner of Christine Avenue in Miami, Queensland � a beachside suburb on the southern Gold Coast. The motel has been family-run since the 1970s. The mural facade on the Gold Coast Highway makes it one of the more recognisable buildings on this stretch of road, and reception is open daily from 9am to 7:30pm.

The Gold Coast is a coastal city in Queensland, Australia � 80 kilometres south of Brisbane and near the New South Wales border. Most international guests arrive via Brisbane Airport (around an hour north on the M1) or Gold Coast Airport at Coolangatta (20 minutes south). The motel is central to the entire coast � beach, theme parks, Broadbeach, Currumbin Wildlife Sanctuary and the national park walk are all within 30 minutes.
