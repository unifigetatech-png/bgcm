Frequently Asked

SEE & DO — Q&A

How far is the motel from the Gold Coast theme parks?

Sea World is around 25 minutes north up the Gold Coast Highway. Warner Bros. Movie World and Wet'n'Wild are both around 30 minutes north on the M1 — they're side by side at Oxenford, so visiting both in one trip just means crossing the car park.

Drive times depend on traffic. Mornings before 9am and evenings after 6pm are typically the easiest. School holidays and Saturdays in summer add 10–20 minutes.

Is it cheaper to stay at Burleigh than at a park hotel?

Yes — significantly. Park-side resorts typically run $300+/night in peak season. Our rooms start at $125 and there are no resort fees, no booking fees, free parking and free Wi-Fi included.

Over a three-night Gold Coast trip that's $400 to $500 back in your pocket — usually enough for an extra park ticket or two and dinner.

Can I do more than one theme park in a day?

Movie World and Wet'n'Wild share a car park at Oxenford. Some visitors split a day between them, though most people find one park per day is plenty — especially with kids.

Sea World is on its own at Main Beach, around 15 minutes south of the other two, so it's typically a full day on its own.

Do you offer theme park transfers or shuttles?

No — we don't run transfers ourselves, and honestly the parks aren't well served by public transport. Free on-site parking at the motel and free parking at the parks make driving the easiest option.

The Route 777 bus runs from a stop nearby to Surfers Paradise (with onward connections), and Stage 3 of the Gold Coast Light Rail is due to open July 2026 with a tram stop close to the motel.

What's there to do without a car?

Plenty. Miami Beach is a 5-minute walk. Burleigh Beach is a flat 6-minute walk (200m). Burleigh Heads National Park, Miami Hill Lookout, Paddock Bakery, Aldi and IGA are all within walking distance.

For longer trips, buses on the Gold Coast Highway run to Surfers Paradise and Coolangatta Airport — Route 777 is the quick express.

What about Dreamworld and other Gold Coast attractions?

Dreamworld is around 35–40 minutes north of the motel on the M1, just past Movie World. We don't have a dedicated page for it yet — but it's an easy drive and shares a region with Movie World and Wet'n'Wild.

Currumbin Wildlife Sanctuary (15 minutes south), the Q1 Skypoint observation deck in Surfers (20 minutes north) and the Tamborine Mountain rainforest walks (45 minutes inland) are also worth a half-day.
