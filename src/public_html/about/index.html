A MOTEL THAT
DOES WHAT IT SAYS

Burleigh Gold Coast Motel has been on this same stretch of the Gold Coast Highway since 1973. The motel has been family-run the whole time — same patch of ground, same commitment to being clean, comfortable and fairly priced.

We're at 1908–1910 Gold Coast Highway in Miami, which puts you 400 metres from Miami Beach, 200m from Burleigh Beach (a flat 6-minute walk), and a straight 30-minute run up the M1 to Movie World, Sea World and Wet'n'Wild.

36 rooms. Outdoor pool. Free parking for cars, utes and trailers. Air conditioning, ceiling fans, microwave, bar fridge and free Wi-Fi in every room. No resort fees, no booking surcharges, no hidden charges at checkout.

Book direct and you get the best rate — simple as that.

GOOD TO KNOW
BEFORE YOU ARRIVE

Check-in is from 2:00 PM and check-out is by 10:00 AM. Reception is staffed daily — if you're arriving after 5:00 PM, call ahead so we can arrange access.

We're a motel, not a resort. You'll get a clean, comfortable room at a fair price, free parking, and a pool. What you won't get is a $40 resort fee on top of the room rate.

Children under 2 stay free in existing bedding. If you need a cot, let us know when you book — we'll sort it out.

We're pet-friendly by arrangement — call reception before booking to confirm availability.
