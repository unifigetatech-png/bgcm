By car

From the airport: Coolangatta is 11 km south, around 15 minutes via Gold Coast Highway. From Brisbane: 90 km north on the M1, allow 75–90 minutes off-peak. Free on-site parking, one permit per room.

🗺️ Google Maps 🍎 Apple Maps

By bus

Route 777 (express) and Route 700 (local) run along the Gold Coast Highway, with a stop a short walk from the motel. Surfers Paradise is 25 minutes north. Coolangatta Airport 25 minutes south. Use a Go Card — fares are lower than cash. Stage 3 of the Gold Coast Light Rail (tram) is due July 2026 with a stop nearby.
